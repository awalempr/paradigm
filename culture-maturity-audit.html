Is Your Culture Built to Scale or Built Around You?

A 20-question diagnostic that reveals if your culture can survive your growth.

This assessment by Paradigm Consulting evaluates the four structural pillars of organizational culture: Clarity, Accountability, Incentive Alignment, and Leadership Stability. It will reveal whether your culture operates as a system or whether it depends entirely on you to function.

This assessment is ideal for founders and operators who:

  • Run businesses generating $100K to $10M annually.
  • Have a team of 3 to 50 and feel like the culture still depends on them personally.
  • Want to scale but are not sure their culture can hold it.
  • Suspect their team underperforms when they are not in the room.

This diagnostic evaluates four structural pillars of culture:

  • Clarity
  • Accountability
  • Incentive Alignment
  • Leadership Stability
  • Whether your team can perform without you in the room.
  • Where your culture creates turnover, confusion, and decision bottlenecks.
  • Which of the four pillars is your weakest and what it is costing you.
  • Whether your culture is scalable, acquirable, and worth owning.
  • A 90-day correction plan based on your exact score and tier.
