This audit by Paradigm Consulting evaluates 6 critical system categories across 18 specific checkpoints. It will reveal where your architecture is producing leverage, where it is creating drag, and what the gaps are costing you annually in unrealized capacity.

This audit is built for founders and operators who:

  • Run businesses generating $250K to $10M annually
  • Suspect their systems are held together by people rather than infrastructure
  • Want to scale but are not sure if their architecture can hold the weight

This audit evaluates six system categories that determine whether your business runs on infrastructure or on you:

  • CRM and Sales — lead tracking, pipeline visibility, sales process
  • Marketing — execution independence, analytics, nurturing automation
  • Delivery — SOPs, onboarding consistency, quality tracking
  • Finance — automated reporting, reconciliation, spending authority
  • Team and Ops — operating rhythm, decision authority, founder independence
  • Technology — tool integration, automation, tech stack governance
  • Which systems are producing leverage and which are producing drag
  • Where founder dependency is hiding inside systems that appear to be working
  • The estimated annual cost of each infrastructure gap
  • A full systems map showing your architecture health across all six categories
  • Exactly what to fix first and what each gap is costing your business
