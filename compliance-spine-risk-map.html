Why this exists

Compliance gaps don't reveal themselves on a Tuesday. They reveal themselves when a regulator calls.

Most scaling service businesses know their compliance posture has gaps. What they don't know is which gaps matter, which gaps are quietly compounding, and what an outside regulator would surface first. The Risk Map is the fastest way to get expert eyes on the operation without committing to a full engagement.

01 · States

Multi-State Exposure

Every state you operate in adds a layer of producer or contractor licensing, anti-rebating rules, disclosure requirements, and worker classification standards. Most operations cannot answer where they are clean and where they are exposed.

02 · Federal

FTC and DOL Surface

ROSCA, FTC Section 5, DOL classification rules, and recordkeeping requirements apply across most categories of recurring service revenue. A single complaint can cascade into a multi-month investigation if the documentation isn't ready.

03 · Speed

You Need a Read in Two Weeks

A full compliance engagement is the right answer for many operations. But you can't commit to a full engagement until you know where the exposure actually is. The Risk Map closes that gap on a defined two-week clock.

What's in the deliverable

A written risk profile, not a verbal summary on a Zoom call.

The Risk Map deliverable is a structured document you can hand to a co-founder, an investor, or outside counsel. The format matters: structural compliance work has to live in writing so it is auditable, actionable, and durable.

9-Category Risk Profile

Your business mapped against the Compliance Spine matrix: three regulatory layers (Federal, State, Operational) times three risk surfaces (Reps, Revenue, Records). Each of the 9 cells gets a rating, a finding, and a specific exposure note.

Top 3 Priority Actions

From the 9-cell map, the three highest-leverage actions you should take in the next 30 days. Each priority includes the gap, the recommended move, and an estimated cost of inaction if it is ignored.

Engagement Decision Note

Honest assessment of whether the gaps surfaced warrant a full 90-day Compliance Spine installation, outside counsel involvement, or a stand-alone fix. No artificial urgency. The Risk Map is built to give you a clear next step regardless of whether you continue with Paradigm.
