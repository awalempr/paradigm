How Exposed Is Your Business?

Discover if your business structure is ready for what comes next.

This assessment by Paradigm Consulting has been carefully curated to ask specific questions as it pertains to your business structure, compliance posture, and operational exposure. It will reveal areas of strength and gaps that, if left unresolved, can directly impact your revenue, valuation, and personal liability.

This assessment is ideal for founders and operators who:

  • Run businesses generating $100K to $10M annually
  • Control or oversee business operations and financial infrastructure
  • Are personally liable or exposed through their business structure
  • Want to scale but are not sure if their foundation can hold it

This diagnostic evaluates four structural areas of business exposure:

  • Legal Structure
  • Financial Controls
  • Data and Security
  • Operational Governance
  • Where your business structure creates personal liability instead of protection
  • The hidden financial leaks that compound as you scale
  • Where a single breach or dispute could shut down operations
  • Which part of your business cannot function without you
  • What is costing you valuation, margin, or both

*If you do not control business operations or manage business infrastructure, this assessment may not be relevant to your role.
